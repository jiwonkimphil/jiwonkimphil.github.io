Jiwon Kim

Practical Philosophy | Metaethics | Philosophy of Language | Normative Ethics | Philosophy of AI

I am a philosopher working in practical philosophy, with research interests in metaethics, philosophy of language, normative ethics, and philosophy of AI.

My work examines moral advice, normative address, standing, uptake, and responsibility in communication. I am especially interested in how speakers and hearers navigate norms governing who may advise, criticise, demand, forgive, blame, or otherwise address others normatively.

Research

My current research focuses on standing in normative address, including cases in which speakers or hearers misattribute who has the standing to make certain normative claims. I also work on questions about artificial intelligence, communication, and responsibility.
